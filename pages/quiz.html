WHICH TECH SECTOR IS THE RIGHT FIT FOR YOU?

It can be very daunting trying to figure out which sector of technology to get into.
So we've designed a quiz to help you figure out the sector that aligns best with your personality
and would possibly be a great fit for you.

Cyber Security

You have a keen eye for detail and enjoy solving puzzles. Cyber security is all about protecting data and systems from threats. If you like thinking critically and staying ahead of challenges, this could be the perfect field for you!

Software

You're a problem solver with a creative mind. Software development involves building apps, websites, and systems that people use every day. If you enjoy logical thinking and bringing ideas to life, this could be your ideal career path!

You love spotting patterns and making sense of information. Data science is about using data to solve problems, predict trends, and make decisions. If you enjoy working with numbers and uncovering insights, this could be your perfect fit!

You're a natural at troubleshooting and helping others. IT professionals keep businesses running smoothly by managing systems, networks, and tech support. If you enjoy hands-on work and problem-solving, this could be the right sector for you!
